About Me

Hello! I'm Bishant K.C., a passionate individual with a keen interest in the world of technology and innovation. Having completed my high school education, I've embarked on a journey fueled by my curiosity and love for computer science and artificial intelligence.

My fascination with the realm of computer science drives me to explore its vast possibilities, from understanding complex algorithms to creating innovative solutions. I have a strong foundation in Python programming language, particularly in building APIs with FastAPI, as well as developing dynamic user interfaces with React.

Beyond the digital realm, I find joy in physical activities such as playing sports, which not only keeps me active but also teaches me the values of teamwork and perseverance. In my downtime, you'll often find me immersed in e-games, honing my strategic skills and engaging with online communities.

Coding isn't just a profession for me; it's a creative outlet through which I express my ideas and bring them to life. Whether it's crafting elegant code structures or designing captivating user interfaces, I thrive on the challenges and opportunities that coding presents.

I'm excited about the future possibilities in technology and am eager to continue learning, growing, and contributing to meaningful projects that make a difference.
